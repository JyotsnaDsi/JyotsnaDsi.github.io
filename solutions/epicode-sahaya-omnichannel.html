Provide Seamless Customer Experience Through Various Channels

Sahaya Omni Channel CX platform is designed to bring together multiple applications supporting different communication channels into an unified platform that can seamlessly integrate and enable seamless transactions across different channels and business applications.



Interact With Customers Through Their Preferred Channel

Many of the business processes that involve in-person customer interaction like retail sales, KYC, physical product demonstrations, payment processing, education, healthcare consultancy etc are being digitised. In order to meet these new challenges, the conventional voice channel based call center is being replaced with an Omnichannel Contact centre to deliver a seamless customer experience in the new hyper digital world.

The Sahaya CX platform allows both customers and customer support executives to interact using the communication channel of their choice. With its inherent Omnichannel capabilities, it allows users to shift between or simultaneously use more than one communication channel to communicate in a reliable and secure manner.
