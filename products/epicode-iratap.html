Epicode IraTap

IraTap, a SIPREC(IETF standard) based call capture solution, is designed to seamlessly integrate with any Session Border Controller (SBC) that supports the SIPREC protocol. This includes widely used platforms such as AudioCodes Mediant, Cisco CUBE, Avaya SBCE, Acme Packets (Oracle), and Ribbon Communications. IraTap supports the recording of both inbound and outbound voice traffic, handling encrypted and unencrypted calls with ease.

Built for robustness and scalability, IraTap features a hyper-scalable architecture that ensures high availability. It can be deployed flexibly across on-premise, hybrid, or pure cloud environments to meet diverse infrastructure needs and business requirements.

IraTap supports simultaneous streaming and recording of media sessions. It can stream these sessions in real time to VoiceAI applications for agent assistance or to other AI-powered tools that rely on real-time voice data. This makes it an ideal solution for environments that require both live processing and secure, compliant recording.
